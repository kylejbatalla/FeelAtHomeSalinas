    Update: June 2020

    After almost 15 years of service, Feel At Home will closing down by the end of 2020 and will no longer accept new patients. We want to thank the families that have trusted us through the years and hope we made a difference in their lives. This website will still remain open for developer purposes.

    Who We Are

    Located in Northeast Salinas, Feel At Home Residential Facility has been family owned and operated for 15+ years. We provide high quality elderly care and living assitance for your loved ones where they will be treated with love, care, and respect. Unlike other larger assisted living facilities that house 100+ residents and caregiver to resident ratios can range anywhere from 1:5 or 1:20, Feel At Home's small family oriented facility ensures that your loved ones will receieve the quality like 1:1 care that they deserve.

    Our small, quiet, and comfortable facility has all the amenities to feel like home. With ambulatory and non-ambulatory care provided, as well as private and semi-private rooms available, we want you to feel comfortable knowing that your loved ones are accounted for and delicately cared for 24/7. Give them the home they deserve, make them feel at home! For a more in-depth perspective of our services provided, please navigate to the "Services" page. For inquiries, pricing, and availability, please contact administrator Normita Batalla via the "Contact Us" page.
