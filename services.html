    Our Services

    24/7 Caregivers

    Our caregivers are available 24/7 to assist with basic needs such as mobility, bedside assistance, medication reminders, and other basic needs around the home.

    Personal Care Assistance

    Whether it's bathing, using the restroom, laundry, or other personal day to day needs, we're here to help.

    Pharmacy Pick-Ups

    Need prescription refills or pickups? One of our staff members will gladly pick up your medication at your pharmacy of choice (Must be located in Salinas).

    Appointment Transportation

    Not available for transportation? Whether it's routine checkups, post-surgery follow ups, or weekly/monthly dialysis, we're here to make sure our residents won't miss an appoinment.
